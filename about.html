A little bit about myself.

My name is Ryan Ke and I am a professional software developer and all around tech enthusiast, usually found trying something new or learning a new skill I've never done before (or being way over my head).

My professional experience is brief, but I pick up topics and adapt quickly, allowing me to take on responsibilities and roles much larger than myself. There is never a time I am not proud of the work I do.

With a focus in web development, I have experience in UI/UX design and Front-end development processes. I am very much a big-picture thinker and enjoy working on products end to end, from ideation all the way to deployment.

My values.

🤩 Passion is everything.

If you don't enjoy what you're doing - move. Channel your energy into something you believe in. You'd be doing yourself and the people working with you a favor. I'm fortunate to be inspired and surrounded by motivated people who enjoy what they do.

📚 Always be learning.

Humans are constant works in progress that need to receive feedback in order to grow. Just like the technologies that we work with, I am always looking at ways to challenge and improve myself, which is why in my spare time I like to participate in coding forums, read blogs, and take on new projects whenever inspiration hits.

🎨 Embrace your creativity.

Everyone needs a creative outlet of some sort - else, we'd go insane! When I'm not designing, I'm compositing photographs, painting digital artworks and helping startups and local businesses realise their potential with design.

🙏 Stay humble.

It doesn't matter whether you're the president, manager, homeless or an average kid on the street. Treat others with respect as you would yourself. We're all human sharing the same wants and needs and facing our own battles everyday. Stay true to yourself and stay true to others.
